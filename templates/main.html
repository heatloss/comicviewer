Frequently Asked Questions

Hey, what's the purpose of this app?
It's to demonstrate how fun and cool it is to have your own comic reading app.
But isn't this already an actual app?
No. A legitimate app would not be built this way. A real comic-reading app would need a centralized database of images, optimized for viewing across a wide range of screens.
It would also have user accounts, presumably hosted on a secure authentication platform.
And, of course, depending on the business model, a real app would also have the ability to process international payments.
Is this app… legal?
Sure, in the same way that Google Images is legal.
So are you gonna make this into a real app?
Not possible. All these comics are hosted on someone else's servers. Any commercial app that uses the content from those servers would need to be owned and operated by that company.
